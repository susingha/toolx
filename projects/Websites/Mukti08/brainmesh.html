LAYOUT

The Contest will consist of Two Rounds The Elimination Round and the Presentation Round. Each group will consist of at most three participants

Round 1

This is the elimination round. The Participants have to devolop their product on one of the SCENARIOS, provided The teams will have to submit An abstract of their design along with
  1. The home page of the design and
  2. 3 screen-shots of their product
  3. All the scenarios for the contest have been proposed by Yahoo!
Ten teams will be selected for the Presentation Round(Round 2).

Round 2

The Selected Teams will participate in the Presentation Round (the Final Round). Each of the selected teams will present their design on stage during MUKTI 07. Each team will be given a time slot of
  1. 10 minutes to present their design, and
  2. 3 minutes for interaction with the judges and the audience.
Separate emphasis will be put on the
  • design
  • theme
  • utility
  • coding
  • browser portability
  • bulkiness and
  • user friendliness of the final product.
