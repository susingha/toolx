Konquer a Server, Make it Serve

Kon-figure ~ System Administration Contest

INTRO

It’s Man rules Computers! We Command and the computer Executes. The better you configure the better it performs. So this is a call to all the techies out there Kreate, Konfigure, Konquer! Let's rock the computers to our Tunes! This Overnight Event gives the participants a chance to Xplore System Administration and takes it to a different level altogether!! So get ready and let the Seventh Symphony play on the Keyboards!

RULES AND REGULATIONS

Each team should consist of a maximum of two participants. Ten teams will be selected for the Final Round from the Elimination Round. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

LAYOUT

Kon-figure is an on-the-spot overnight System Administration competition. The competition will be held in two stages both of which will be held at NIT Durgapur during MUKTI ‘07

Round 1

This will be a written elimination round. It will contain objective questions. The duration will be declared on the day of the contest at the venue.

Round 2

Ten teams will be selected and will be given one computer and one night to solve a set of problems. The number and nature of the problems and the evaluation criteria will be revealed at the venue, depending on the problems. The participants will be allowed to take in any book or material along with them.

SKILLS SET REQUIRED

Knowledge on linux commands, configuration files, basic networking and OS concepts. Google is your biggest friend.
