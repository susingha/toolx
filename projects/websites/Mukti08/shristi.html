Let's INNOVATE!

Srishti ~ The Software Design Contest!

INTRO

It's often said that Human Beings learn through Observation. We at Linux Users’ Group provide this unique platform to Simulate the Actuality and Precision of the Software World in its Totality.


The Software Industry is always on the move, Innovating or adding new Dimensions to the existing concepts. Creating something new is the Order of the day. This event will give the participants an opportunity to Create something new using their basic knowledge of C Programming.


An Overnight Event, this is the Brainchild of Linux Users’ Group which is an addition to MUKTI this year. It’s all about exploring and giving Shape to a new product altogether… all in all, it’s nothing but “Srishti”!

RULES AND REGULATIONS

Each team should consist of a maximum of three participants – two participants for coding and one for promotion of the software. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

LAYOUT

Srishti is an on-the-spot software design competition. The competition will be held in three stages.

Round 1

This will be an elimination round. It will contain objective questions. The duration will be declared on the day of the contest at the venue.

Round 2

This will be the coding round to be conducted in C-Language. Each team will be provided with a computer loaded with Red Hat Linux 9.0. The number and nature of the problems and the evaluation criteria will be revealed at the venue, depending on the problems.

Round 3

In this round, the manager of the team has to sell(market) the finished product.

SKILLS SET REQUIRED

Proficiency in coding in the C-Language. An idea about the basic data structures. Good communication skills and publicizing ability (for promotion of the designed software)
