In-C-anity ~ The C-Programming Contest!

INTRO

Codes Cumulatively Command a Computer. And when it's OPEN SOURCE, it's even better! A chance to become a Programmer and steal the limelight is on offer at MUKTI '08. Devising new codes in C, using Linux as a backdrop, programming is now OPEN for all!!

RULES AND REGULATIONS

Each team should consist of a maximum of two participants. Ten teams will be selected for the Final Round from the Elimination Round. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

LAYOUT

In-C-anity is an on-the-spot C-Programming competition. The competition will be held in two stages both of which will be held at NIT Durgapur during MUKTI ‘07

Round 1

This will be a written elimination round. It will contain objective questions. The duration will be declared on the day of the contest at the venue.

Round 2

Ten teams will be selected, who will be given one computer and one night to solve a set of problems based on C-Language. Each team will be provided with one computer loaded with Red Hat Linux 9.0. The number and nature of the problems and the evaluation criteria will be revealed at the venue, depending on the problems. The participants will be allowed to take in any standard C book.

SKILLS SET REQUIRED

Proficiency in coding in the C-Language. An idea about the basic data structures.
